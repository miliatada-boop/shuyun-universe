# Shuyun Universe VI Reference

Use this reference for Xiaohongshu covers in the Shuyun Universe style.

## Palette

- Chalk: `#F5F2EE`
- Sand veil: `#E8E2D8`
- Warm drift: `#C4B49A`
- Mist: `#9EA8B2`
- Sage tide: `#7B8E82`
- Deep current: `#4A5C6B`
- Abyssal: `#1C2B35`
- Lacquer: `#6B2737`
- Cinnabar: `#9B3A40`
- Antique gold: `#B8955A`
- Pale gold: `#D4BA7E`

## Typography

- Serif display feel: Didot, Baskerville, Cormorant Garamond, or similar.
- Sans label feel: Avenir Next, DM Sans, or similar.
- Chinese: use a clean medium-weight sans or refined Song/Hei pairing depending on system fonts.
- Main Chinese title should be large, confident, and easy to read on mobile.
- Small English/source labels can use wide tracking, but never let them compete with the main title.

## Layout Principles

- Make the first visual read within one second on a phone.
- Use one dominant message in one or two Chinese lines.
- Keep source/person/video information small.
- Avoid dense multi-section layouts for covers.
- Use generous whitespace and fine gold/lacquer rules sparingly.
- Use soft vertical gradients. Avoid hard horizontal bands.
- Keep gradients subtle enough that they feel like light, not a block.
- If placing a frame image near the top, leave a clear gap before top labels.
- Do not let top-left labels overlap the image.

## Proven Cover Template

For the current preferred style:

- Background: top `sand` into lower `chalk` with a long natural vertical gradient.
- Image: centered video frame in the upper half, softened at the bottom with a chalk fade.
- Header: tiny lacquer rule, `SHUYUN UNIVERSE`, and speaker name.
- Title: large dark `abyssal`, then one smaller `lacquer` line.
- Footer: a thin antique gold rule and small deep-current source line.
