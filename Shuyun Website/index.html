我在法国生活了八年，在巴黎策划了三十余个跨文化项目——从卢浮宫非遗展到孚日广场行为艺术周，从高定发布到国际社区建设。 I lived in France for eight years, curating over thirty cross-cultural projects in Paris — from Louvre heritage exhibitions to Place des Vosges performance art weeks, from haute couture launches to international community building.
我相信，很多真正有价值的创始人和品牌，正在被错误的价格、错误的语言、错误的市场位置所掩盖。我做的事，是帮他们找到真正属于自己的位置——在正确的世界里，被正确的人看见。 I believe many truly valuable founders and brands are being obscured by wrong pricing, wrong language, and wrong market positioning. What I do is help them find their rightful place — seen by the right people, in the right world.
