01 · The real problem

问题不是没人关注，
而是关注没有变成长期关系。

很多Founder已经有内容、有用户、有信任感。真正卡住的是：用户来了以后，没有清晰的沉淀路径、产品阶梯与团队执行系统。

The work is not about adding another group, campaign, or content calendar. It is about building a relationship infrastructure that helps the right users stay, deepen trust, move through the right offers, and become part of a long-term business system.

For high-trust founders, growth cannot be separated from credibility. The system has to protect the founder's time, maintain the quality of the community, and make the next step obvious for the user.

这不是“帮你拉群”，也不是“提高社群活跃”。真正要解决的是：用户如何被识别、被分层、被长期照顾，并在合适的时机进入合适的产品关系。
