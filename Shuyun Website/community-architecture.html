01 · What is Cloud Circle

这不是一个社群。
这是一个高信任Founder关系场。

Cloud Circle 是一个长期同行网络，一个创始人成长环境，也是高质量机会自然发生的信任场域。

Cloud Circle exists for founders, creators, and cultural entrepreneurs who are building with depth. It is designed for people who need more than visibility: they need peers, context, trust, and a room where serious conversations can happen without performance.

The circle connects high-perception founders, healing and women's growth IPs, international creators, community builders, aesthetic entrepreneurs, and long-termist brands.

它连接的是高感知Founder、疗愈IP、女性成长品牌、国际化创作者、社群主理人、审美型创业者与长期主义品牌。这里不是热闹的流量池，而是能被长期信任、长期推荐、长期同行的人群系统。
